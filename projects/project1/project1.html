Web Site Critiques

Questions

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

JL Home Evaluation

  1. My first impression of this website is that it is super busy. The title of the page has highlighter colors that dont seem to match what she is trying to sell.
  2. The organization on this website seems to be very chaotic. It almost seems like each section that she has displayed on her website should be a different website altogether. She has "Galactic Spirit" and then another one that is labled "interior design". The only thing that I see that links all of these pages together is that it has the same lady as the owner.
  3. The site structure is confusing because I do not see how the other pages are related.
  4. the multi-media destracts from the overall page. There are so many pictures on the page and I don't understand how they relate to what she is advertising.
  5. The home page loaded really quick, which I was really suprised by since she has so many graphics and YouTube links that I thought it would be very slow.
  6. Text, Color, and size all seemed to be distrating to me. The background clolors on the "Chakra &Color Triumphs" had a very bright background which made the small text very hard to read.
  7. Yes, it had a navigation bar that would have drop downs once you hovered over the word, and they seem to function fine.
  8. Yes, the color sceme seemed to be crazy and hard to look at through out the website. It was consistantly bad.
  9. When loading the website to my phone, it immidately tells me that I should switch to "landscape mode", I don't find that very professional and that seems to be a work around.
  10. The entire website is unusual. I don't feel like I would have ever purposly went to this website on my everyday scrolling. She does seem to have everything catagorized into its own unique place. However, I cant tell if she wants to tell my astrological signs or sell me skin cream.
